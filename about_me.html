About Me

I am currently a first year Ph.D. student in Astrophysics at Vanderbilt University in Nashville, Tennessee. In May of 2023, I graduated with a Bachelor's of Science in Physics from Denison University in Granville, Ohio. I am broadly interested in black holes, their formation, and their effect on their galactic environment. In my not-so-free time, I love to rollerblade, crochet, figure draw, and take ballet classes. While at Denison University, I also studied music composition, and you can find my pieces on my music page.
